MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)

Become a doctor equipped to diagnose and treat diverse illnesses. Specialize in areas like general practice, surgery, or internal medicine. This demanding yet rewarding path allows you to directly impact lives and contribute to advancements in healthcare.

BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)

Focus on oral health with expertise in diagnosis, treatment, and prevention. Pursue careers as a dentist, oral surgeon, or orthodontist. Master the art of maintaining smiles and ensuring oral well-being for your patients.

B.Sc. Nursing

Develop the knowledge and skills for patient care in hospitals or community settings. Specialize as a Registered Nurse (RN), midwife, or public health nurse. Make a direct impact on patient well-being, providing compassionate care and support throughout their journeys.

Pharmacy Programs

Gain expertise in pharmaceutical sciences, from drug development to dispensing medication. Pursue careers as pharmacists, pharmaceutical researchers, or drug development specialists. Contribute to scientific advancements and ensure safe and effective medication use for patients.

Physiotherapy Programs

Master muscle and skeletal rehabilitation, helping patients regain mobility and manage pain. Become a physiotherapist, sports therapist, or pain management specialist. Improve lives through physical therapy interventions and empower individuals to reach their full potential.
